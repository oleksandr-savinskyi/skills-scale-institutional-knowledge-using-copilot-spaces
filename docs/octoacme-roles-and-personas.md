# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Collaborates with PM and Product Managers to define user workflows, creates wireframes and prototypes, gathers user feedback, and ensures usability standards are met.

### Responsibilities
- Define and validate user workflows and interaction patterns
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes for features
- Gather and synthesize user feedback through research and usability testing
- Ensure usability and accessibility standards are met
- Collaborate with Developers to ensure design intent is implemented correctly

### Goals
- Ensure the product is intuitive, accessible, and meets user needs
- Reduce rework caused by late-stage UX issues
- Advocate for the end user throughout the project lifecycle

### Typical Communication
- Participates in iteration planning, backlog grooming, and sprint demos
- Shares design artifacts (wireframes, prototypes) via design tools or repo
- Works closely with Developers and QA during implementation and validation

---

## Technical Writer / Documentation Specialist

### Role Summary
Maintains technical and process documentation; partners with Developers and PMs to ensure docs stay current, accurate, and actionable; supports onboarding and knowledge sharing.

### Responsibilities
- Author and maintain technical guides, API docs, and process documentation
- Partner with Developers and PMs to harvest and clarify information
- Review and update docs at each release to reflect changes
- Support onboarding by ensuring documentation is accessible and well-structured
- Own the documentation review checklist as part of the Definition of Done

### Goals
- Reduce single-person dependency risk through comprehensive documentation
- Accelerate onboarding and enable self-service knowledge access
- Maintain a single source of truth for processes and technical details

### Typical Communication
- Coordinates with all roles to gather information
- Participates in sprint reviews to capture what changed
- Publishes documentation updates alongside releases

---

## QA Analyst / Tester

### Role Summary
Defines test plans, executes test cases, reports defects, and verifies acceptance criteria; participates early in planning to identify risks; supports regression testing after changes.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test plans, test cases, and regression suites
- Execute functional, integration, and smoke tests
- Report, track, and verify defects to closure
- Participate in planning to identify testability risks early
- Validate that acceptance criteria are met before release

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and reliability at every release
- Shift testing left to catch issues earlier in the cycle
- Maintain high test coverage and minimize production incidents

### Typical Communication
- Partners with Developers during implementation for early feedback
- Reports test results and defect status to PM and Product Manager
- Participates in release go/no-go decisions

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Elicits business needs, transforms requirements into actionable specifications, translates between business and technical teams, and analyzes impact and process improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit, document, and validate business and functional requirements
- Create user stories, process flows, and acceptance criteria
- Analyze the impact of proposed changes on existing processes
- Bridge communication between stakeholders, Product Managers, and Developers
- Identify opportunities for process improvement and efficiency gains

### Goals
- Ensure requirements are clear, complete, and actionable before development begins
- Reduce ambiguity and rework caused by misunderstood requirements
- Align business needs with technical implementation

### Typical Communication
- Facilitates requirements workshops with stakeholders
- Collaborates closely with Product Managers and Developers during planning
- Produces and maintains requirements documentation in the project repo

---

## Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) / DevOps

### Role Summary
Maintains CI/CD pipelines, monitors system health, manages production incidents, and implements automation for reliability and operational efficiency.

### Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment infrastructure
- Monitor production systems and respond to incidents
- Define and track SLOs/SLAs and reliability metrics
- Implement automation to reduce toil and improve deployment reliability
- Consult during planning and release stages for operational readiness

### Goals
- Maximize system availability, reliability, and performance
- Reduce deployment risk through automation and standardized pipelines
- Enable rapid, safe delivery to production

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Developers during design and release planning
- Coordinates with PM during release windows and incident response
- Provides post-incident reports and contributes to retrospectives
